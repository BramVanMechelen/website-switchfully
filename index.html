Who am I?

Hi there, welcome to my first webpage! This page is about something quite special and unique: Me! I grew up in a little township, part of a little village, which in itself was part of a small town. Just like every country boy, I wanted to move to the big city when I grew up. I was also fascinated by history. I wasn't the best student, don't know much about the French I took, but cool facts and dates always stuck. Quite a lot has changed since then: I moved from the small township to the city, I got myself a history degree and soon I will start a new career path: as a software developer! - To be continued...

Career

Teacher

After getting my history degree, I start studying to become a teacher. For the last two years I have been a teacher at several high schools in the Antwerp region. Not only have I taught history, but also Dutch, religion and tourism.

Student

After high school I started studying at the university of Antwerp. I chose Antwerp because it was close by and they focused more on modern history than Ghent or Leuven. I really enjoyed studying history and met a lot of great and interesting people. During my studies I was also part of the organization of the history student club. In my final year I wrote my thesis on the repatriation of dead bodies to their home country; specifically about the Dutch soldiers who died in Antwerp during the Belgian Revolution.
